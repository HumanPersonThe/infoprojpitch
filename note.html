Job Overview

This release delivers a complete overhaul of the disclosure fee calculation engine within the Loan Origination System. The previous implementation had accumulated technical debt over three major versions and could not accommodate variable-rate ARM products or complex lender credit structures.

The new engine introduces a modular calculation pipeline that separates rate-dependent fees from static fees, enabling accurate disclosure generation across all currently offered UHM loan products including conventional, FHA, VA, and USDA programs.

Business Impact: Disclosure accuracy improved from an estimated 91% to 99.6% across test scenarios. Regulatory compliance with TRID timing requirements is now enforced at the system level, reducing manual reviewer workload.

Logic & Process

The disclosure engine processes each loan through the following calculation pipeline:

1
Product Type Resolution
System identifies loan product (Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, ARM) and loads the corresponding fee schedule and regulatory thresholds.
2
Rate-Dependent Fee Calculation
Variable fees (origination, discount points) are calculated against the current locked or floating rate. ARM products calculate against initial rate and each subsequent cap adjustment.
3
Lender Credit Application
If a lender credit is present, it is applied to reduce itemized fees in priority order per CFPB guidance, never reducing fees below zero.
4
APR & Finance Charge Computation
Annual Percentage Rate and total finance charges are computed from the net fee schedule. Values are flagged if they exceed HOEPA thresholds.
5
Disclosure Document Generation
Loan Estimate or Closing Disclosure is generated and timestamped. TRID 3-day and 7-day waiting period timers are initialized automatically.
