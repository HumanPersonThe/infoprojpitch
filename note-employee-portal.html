Job Overview

The benefits enrollment module received a full visual and accessibility overhaul in this release. The previous interface had grown organically over several years and had accumulated UX debt â€” inconsistent form patterns, poor keyboard navigation, and missing ARIA labels that failed WCAG 2.1 AA standards. This release resolves all outstanding accessibility audit findings and introduces a modernized step-by-step enrollment wizard.

Business Impact: Reduced benefits enrollment support tickets by an estimated 35%. All 47 previously flagged WCAG violations have been resolved.

Logic & Process
1
Enrollment Wizard Flow
Employees now complete benefits selection through a 4-step wizard: Plan Selection ? Dependent Coverage ? Cost Summary ? Confirmation. Each step validates required fields before advancing.
2
Accessibility Compliance
All form inputs now include proper aria-label, aria-describedby, and role attributes. Keyboard navigation follows WCAG 2.1 focus order guidelines. Color contrast ratios meet 4.5:1 minimum for all text elements.
3
Workday Sync
On confirmation, enrollment elections are synced to Workday in real time via the existing Workday Bridge integration. A retry queue handles transient sync failures with up to 3 automatic retries.
